Delicious Nepali Momo Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup minced chicken (or vegetables)
  • 1 medium onion (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Spices (turmeric, chili powder, pepper)
  • Fresh coriander (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the dough: Mix flour, a little oil, and water. Knead into a soft dough and rest for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Make the filling: Mix minced chicken/veggies with onion, ginger-garlic, spices, salt, and a little oil.
  3. Shape the momos:
    • Roll small dough balls into thin circles.
    • Add 1 spoon of filling and fold into desired shape.
  4. Steam: Place momos in a steamer and cook for 10–12 minutes until shiny.
  5. Serve: Enjoy hot with momo chutney!

Tips

  • Add finely chopped cabbage for extra softness.
  • Make the dough neither too hard nor too soft.
  • Don’t overfill the momos; they will break.
